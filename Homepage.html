Welcome

Jyotipunj Secondary Boarding School stands as a beacon of excellence in education, fostering a holistic approach to learning that goes beyond academics. Founded on principles of integrity, compassion, and academic rigor, the school provides a nurturing environment where students can thrive intellectually, socially, and emotionally.


At Jyotipunj, the curriculum is designed to not only impart knowledge but also to instill critical thinking skills, creativity, and a sense of responsibility towards society. The dedicated faculty members are committed to inspiring and guiding students towards academic excellence while also nurturing their individual talents and interests


Beyond the classroom, Jyotipunj offers a vibrant array of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, music, and community service initiatives. These activities play a crucial role in fostering teamwork, leadership skills, and a well-rounded personality.
